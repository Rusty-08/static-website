PLAY-TO-EARN

Play-to-earn is a business model that works on blockchain technology. Put simply, players can play a game and potentially earn cryptocurrency. In play-to-earn games, players can get a hold of potentially valuable in-game assets. That can be anything from skins or cards to a specific type of cryptocurrency.
